Creating a standout resume is easier with AI. In 2026, AI resume builders can write, format, and optimize your resume for applicant tracking systems. Here are the best options.

Best AI Resume Builders

Rezi AI

  • ATS optimization – Pass automated screenings
  • AI writer – Generate bullet points
  • Keyword targeting – Match job descriptions
  • Free tier – Basic features free

Price: Free-$29/mo | Best for: ATS optimization

Kickresume

  • AI writer – Generate resume content
  • Templates – 35+ professional designs
  • Cover letters – AI-generated letters
  • Website builder – Personal portfolio site

Price: Free-$19/mo | Best for: Design-focused resumes

Teal

  • Job matching – Compare resume to jobs
  • AI writer – Improve bullet points
  • Job tracker – Manage applications
  • Free – Generous free tier

Price: Free-$9/mo | Best for: Job search management

ChatGPT for Resumes

  • Free – No cost option
  • Flexible – Any format you want
  • Cover letters – Write both
  • Customization – Tailor to any job

Price: Free | Best for: DIY approach

Free AI Resume Options

  • Rezi Free – 1 resume, basic AI
  • Kickresume Free – Basic templates
  • Teal Free – Most features free
  • ChatGPT Free – Write your own

Conclusion

For AI resume builders in 2026, Rezi is best for ATS optimization, Kickresume for design, and Teal for the overall job search. For free, use ChatGPT to write content and format it yourself.
