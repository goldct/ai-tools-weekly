Podcasting is easier than ever with AI. In 2026, AI tools for podcasters handle recording, editing, transcription, and even marketing. Here are the best options.

AI Tools for Every Podcast Stage

Recording

  • Riverside.fm – Remote recording with AI enhancement
  • Zoom AI – Built-in recording and transcription
  • Descript – Record directly with AI cleanup

Editing

  • Descript – Edit audio by editing text
  • Adobe Podcast – AI audio enhancement
  • Auphonic – Automatic leveling and noise reduction

Transcription

  • Descript – Built-in transcription with editing
  • Otter.ai – Accurate transcription ($17/mo)
  • Whisper – OpenAI's free transcription model

Content Repurposing

  • Opus Clip – Create short clips from episodes
  • Headliner – Audiograms for social media
  • ChatGPT – Generate show notes and blog posts

Best All-in-One: Descript

Descript is the top choice for most podcasters:

  • Text-based editing – Delete words to delete audio
  • Overdub – Clone your voice for fixes
  • Studio Sound – AI audio enhancement
  • Transcription included

Price: Free-$24/mo

Free AI Podcasting Tools

  • Audacity + Whisper – Free recording + transcription
  • ChatGPT – Generate show notes free
  • Canva Free – Create cover art
  • Descript Free – 1 hour transcription/month

Conclusion

For podcasters in 2026, Descript offers the best all-in-one solution. Use Adobe Podcast for audio enhancement and Opus Clip for content repurposing. Start with free tools and upgrade as your podcast grows.
