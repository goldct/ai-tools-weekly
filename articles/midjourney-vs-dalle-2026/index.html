Midjourney v7 - Best for Artistic Images

Midjourney continues to lead in artistic quality:

  • Stunning aesthetics – Most beautiful AI images
  • Style consistency – Great for series of images
  • Character reference – Consistent characters across images
  • Higher resolution – Native 4K output
  • Discord-based – Requires Discord app

Best for: Concept art, illustrations, creative projects.

DALL-E 4 - Best for Photorealism & Ease

DALL-E 4 excels at photorealistic images:

  • Photorealistic quality – Nearly indistinguishable from photos
  • Text rendering – Accurate text in images
  • Simple interface – Just type and generate
  • Inpainting/outpainting – Edit images easily
  • No subscription – Pay only for what you use

Best for: Product images, marketing, photorealistic needs.

When to Choose Midjourney

  • You need artistic, stylized images
  • You're creating concept art or illustrations
  • You want consistent style across many images
  • You're comfortable with Discord

When to Choose DALL-E

  • You need photorealistic images
  • You need text in your images
  • You prefer a simple web interface
  • You want to pay per-use, not subscribe

Pricing Comparison (2026)

  • Midjourney – $10/mo (Basic) to $60/mo (Pro)
  • DALL-E 4 – ~$0.04 per image, no subscription

For occasional use, DALL-E is cheaper. For heavy use, Midjourney offers better value.

Conclusion

For artistic work, choose Midjourney. For photorealism and ease, choose DALL-E. Many creators use both: Midjourney for concept art, DALL-E for product images.
